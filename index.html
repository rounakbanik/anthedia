Social Media Specialist


Responsibilities:

  • Build and execute social media strategy through competitive research, platform determination, benchmarking, messaging and audience identification
  • Generate, edit, publish, manage and share content (original text, images, video or HTML) that builds meaningful connections and encourages community members to take action

Requirements

  • Excellent consulting, writing, editing (photo/video/text), presentation and communication skills
  • Working experience in social media marketing or as a digital media specialist is a plus
  • Must demonstrable social networking experience and social analytics tools knowledge, tools like (Google adwords, YouTube analytics, and other similar tools)
  • Knowledge of online marketing and good understanding of major marketing channels
